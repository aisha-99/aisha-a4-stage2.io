About Us

Discover Our Legacy

Welcome to Ava Velvette, where luxury meets craftsmanship. Our journey began 43 years ago with a passion for creating timeless accessories that transcend trends. Today, we stand as a symbol of elegance, blending tradition with innovation. Explore our story, meet the artisans behind each masterpiece, and join us on a voyage through the artistry that defines Ava Velvette.

Our Vision

At Ava Velvette, our vision transcends the boundaries of fashion, epitomizing a commitment to timeless elegance. We envision a world where sophistication is not just a style choice but a way of life. Ava Velvette stands as a beacon of refined luxury, dedicated to crafting accessories that seamlessly blend classic allure with contemporary grace. Our vision is to empower individuals to embrace their uniqueness, celebrating every moment with accessories that resonate with enduring beauty.


Craftsmanship

Ava Velvette stands as a testament to the artistry of craftsmanship. Each product within our collection reflects the painstaking dedication to detail and precision that defines our brand. Our artisans, with their skilled hands and unwavering commitment, transform raw materials into exquisite masterpieces. We take pride in the meticulous craftsmanship that permeates every stitch, ensuring that each Ava Velvette creation is not just an accessory but a manifestation of unparalleled artistry.


Founding Principles

Guided by principles of sophistication, authenticity, and innovation, Ava Velvette is built on a foundation of unwavering commitment to our customers. Our founding principles dictate every facet of our brand – from design philosophy to ethical practices. We believe in creating accessories that stand the test of time, in sustainability, and in fostering a sense of community. These principles serve as our compass, directing Ava Velvette towards creating enduring beauty with purpose.
